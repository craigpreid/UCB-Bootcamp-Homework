Correlation between Poverty and Physical Activity

There is an increasing trend in poverty with decreasing physical activity in the United States. Perhaps, lack of physical fitness prevents people from going out and earning money. Puerto Rico is an outlier with a strong inverse relationship between poverty and physical activity.

The data was obtained from the U.S. Census Bureau's American Community and the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System for the year 2014.
